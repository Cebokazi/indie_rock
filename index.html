What's an Indie Rock?

It is a genre of rock music that is originated in the United sttes and the United Kingdom in the 1970s and is best described as independent record labels. The term became associated with the music they produced and was initially used interchangeably with alternative rock or "guitar pop rock".

Pixies

Pixies are an American alternative rock band founded in 1986 in Boston, Massachusetts. It was formed by university students Charles Micheal Kitteridge Thompson IV (vocals/guitar) and Joseph Alberto Santiago (guitar). Later Kimberly Deal and David Lovering were introduced in the band.

This pair decided to publish an ad calling for musicians to form a "Husker Du/Peter, Paul & Mary band" after checking outthe local club scene. They attracted a huge European audience as well as moderate underground American. They secured a recording contract with the UK independent label 4AD Records on strengths of a series od superior demo tapes recorded with local producer Gary Smith.
